Computer assisted Language Learning

Welcome to the computer assisted language learning website. I am providing a number of automated tools to make life easier for language teachers and language learners. The current featured content is an automated article writer for teachers who want to reinforce topics or for students wo want to practice their language skils by reading about topic that interest them Just choose a genre from the drop down menu and type in your topic of choice. The system works best with simple noun/gerund topics such as "Pizza", "Making pizza", "The history of pizza". See the About page for further information.
